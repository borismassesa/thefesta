# Wedding-Focused Carousel Redesign - COMPLETE

## Summary
Completely redesigned the splash screen carousel to align with The Knot and WeddingWire's approach, focusing on actual wedding planning core features instead of generic poetic content. The carousel now clearly communicates the real value proposition of The Festa application.

## What Was Changed

### **Before: Generic Poetic Content**
1. "Where Dreams Begin" - Vague, no specific feature
2. "Artistry in Motion" - Unclear what this means
3. "Seamless Symphony" - Too abstract
4. "Moments That Matter" - No actionable information
5. "Your Story Awaits" - Generic call to action

### **After: Real Wedding Planning Features**
1. **"Find Your Dream Venue"** - Venue discovery with verified reviews
2. **"Connect with Vendors"** - Photographer, caterers, florists marketplace
3. **"Manage Your Guest List"** - Digital invitations & RSVP tracking
4. **"Plan Your Budget"** - Expense tracking & budget management
5. **"Perfect Timeline"** - Timeline creation with checklists & reminders

## Core Features Highlighted

### **1. Venue Discovery (Slide 1)**
- **Feature**: Venue marketplace
- **Value**: "Discover beautiful venues across Tanzania with verified reviews and photos"
- **Context**: Matches The Knot's venue search feature
- **Swahili**: "Tafuta Mahali Pa Ndoto"

### **2. Vendor Marketplace (Slide 2)**
- **Feature**: Professional vendor directory
- **Value**: "Photographers, caterers, florists, and more - all verified professionals"
- **Context**: Core feature from both The Knot and WeddingWire
- **Swahili**: "Unganisha na Wauzaji"

### **3. Guest Management (Slide 3)**
- **Feature**: Digital invitations & RSVP system
- **Value**: "Send digital invitations, track RSVPs, and manage your guest list effortlessly"
- **Context**: Essential wedding planning tool
- **Swahili**: "Simamia Orodha ya Wageni"

### **4. Budget Planning (Slide 4)**
- **Feature**: Budget tracker & expense management
- **Value**: "Track expenses, set budgets, and manage payments with smart tools"
- **Context**: Critical feature for wedding planning
- **Swahili**: "Panga Bajeti Yako"

### **5. Timeline & Organization (Slide 5)**
- **Feature**: Timeline builder with checklists
- **Value**: "Create detailed timelines and stay organized with checklists and reminders"
- **Context**: Helps couples stay on track
- **Swahili**: "Muda wa Kufaa"

## Learning from The Knot & WeddingWire

### **Key Insights Applied:**
1. **Feature-Focused Messaging**: Each slide highlights a specific, actionable feature
2. **Clear Value Proposition**: Users immediately understand what they can do
3. **Professional Language**: Clear, direct communication over poetic abstraction
4. **Real Wedding Planning Needs**: Addresses actual pain points
5. **Verification & Trust**: Emphasizes verified vendors and reviews

### **The Knot's Approach:**
- Clear feature categories (venues, vendors, registry, etc.)
- Direct messaging about what users can accomplish
- Focus on practical wedding planning tools
- Professional, trustworthy tone

### **WeddingWire's Approach:**
- Vendor marketplace emphasis
- Review and rating system
- Budget and guest management tools
- Practical planning features

## Design Improvements

### **1. Clear Communication**
- **Before**: Abstract, poetic titles that don't explain features
- **After**: Direct, feature-focused titles that immediately communicate value

### **2. Actionable Content**
- **Before**: "Orchestrate every detail with precision and grace"
- **After**: "Create detailed timelines and stay organized with checklists and reminders"

### **3. Trust Building**
- **Before**: No mention of verification or trust
- **After**: "verified professionals", "verified reviews and photos"

### **4. Specific Features**
- **Before**: Generic descriptions
- **After**: Specific tools (RSVPs, budget tracking, checklists, etc.)
